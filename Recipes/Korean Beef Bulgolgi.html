Korean Beef Bulgolgi

Making authentic Korean Beef Bulgolgi using cheap ingredients

Ingredients

Korean Beef Ingredients
1 1/2 lbs. Flank Steak (or any marbled cut)
Vegetable Oil
Green Onions
Marinade Ingredients
Soy sauce
Seasame Oil
Light brown sugar
Canola oil
Freshly minced garlic and ginger
Asian pear
Red pepper flakes

Once you have your ingredients we can begin the process of making a authentic Koeran Beef Bulgolgi

Steps

  1. First, slice the beef as thinly as you can, then set aside in a large bowl. Using frozen or cold beef will make the slicing easier.
  2. To make the marinade, in a blender or food processor, blend the onion chunks, garlic, pear, green onion pieces, brown sugar, pepper, soy sauce, and sesame oil until smooth. Marinade the beef overnight.
  3. Then, heat the canola oil in a large skillet over high heat. Pat the meat dry.
  4. Sear the marinated beef and onions until browned, without crowding the pan. Sprinkle with the sliced green onions and sesame seeds.
  5. Serve with rice and sides. Enjoy!
