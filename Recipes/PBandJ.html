Classic PB and J

Making a great tasting PB and J using standard ingredients

Ingredients

  • Strawberry Jelly
  • Peanut Butter
  • White Bread

Once you have your ingredients we can begin the process of making a delicious PB and J

Steps

  1. First, lay out the ingredients in front of you.
  2. Then, on one slice of bread, spread peanut butter evenly over the bread.
  3. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread.
  4. Next, put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in.
  5. Enjoy!
